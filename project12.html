Multiverse

Video Production/3D Animation/Editing

Multiverse is an animation independent group project. We, six college students from Ming Chung University, wrote the Multiverse story, filmed the footage, composited and converted it into 2D art style animation.

I was in charge of 3D modeling - landscape and spaceship, original footage editing and credit animation.

Story Abstract

Franco is a scientist who devotes his lifetime researching the multiverse theory. He believes the 超感知藥劑 can link human's sensation with someone in the multiverse. One day in his dream, he saw a few blurry shadows of himself on the other planet. The planet is about to explode. The explosion’s impact wakes Franco up in the middle of the night. He writes down the missing formula for travel between multiverse and injects the 超感知藥劑.
