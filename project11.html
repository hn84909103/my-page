Hamster Run

Game Design/Unity

Hamster Run is a single-player 3D adventure game. The player will be the pet hamster, and the player needs to explore the human house to collect all the sunflower seeds. The game will have three levels, and to unlock the next level, the player needs to gather a certain number of sunflower seeds.

Description

Player will be the hamster and explore the human house.

  • Goals
    • Collect the sunflower seeds
    • Not encounter with humans (enemies!)
    • Return to the cage in time
  • 3 Levels
  • Health
    • Find the “crumb” to recover player’s health
  • 5 minutes each round

Game Mechanics
  • Controls
    • W, S, D, A keys for player movement control
    • O, P keys or left, right mouse button - camera view control
    • Space bar for jumping
  • Rounds
    • Each round the timer starts at 5 minutes and counts down to 0
    • Player meets the requirement, unlocks the next level
  • Score Points
    • Each level requires a certain amount of seeds
    • Level 1 (10), level 2 (15), level 3 (30)
  • Health Points
    • Full health bar is 100 pts
    • During the game, the health point will decrease slowly (-0.16 pts/per sec)
    • Player can increase the HP by collecting the crumb item (+10Hp)
  • Enemy
    • Humans are the enemies
    • Three types of enemies: the kid, the elder brother, and the mom
    • Player loses 30 HP when encountered the enemies
  • Game Ends
    • Win
      • The player collected the required amount of the seeds from all three rooms and got back to the cage.
    • Lose
      • The player didn’t get back to the cage in time. Or, the player’s health points reached 0.
