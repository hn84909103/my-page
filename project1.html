Semiotics of the Bathroom

An Artwork inspired by Martha Rosler: "Semiotics of the Kitchen" (1975)

Semiotics of the Bathroom is a project under AFTER HOUR production. AFTER HOUR production is established by three members, Yu-Chen, Amber and myself. My role in the team is the Art Director / Videographer.

Story Behind the Scene

We, three Taiwanese visited SUNY New Paltz Samuel Dorsky Museum of Art on a road trip during the 2017 Labor Day weekend, and we saw Martha Rosler’s “Semiotics of the Kitchen” (1975). The A-Z of the kitchen, these are the common symbols for western society. In Taiwan, the common symbol we use is called Bopomofo which has 37 symbols. We use the items in the bathroom to introduce each Bopomofo symbol.
