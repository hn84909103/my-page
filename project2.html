Neon Sign of Rooster Year

3D Animation

Using the traditional chinese character 雞 which means rooster as the based for the neon light.

Red color is the main theme color in the Lunar New Year and it has the meaning - wish you good luck.

-

First I created the typography in illustrator. Based on the 2D typography, building the 3D model. The 3D model is created in C4D and edited in Adobe After Effects.
